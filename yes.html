Trouble on the Horizon

Ye scrubbed the deck, served the meat and rum. A long day's work to prove your worth to sea Dogs old and young. Up on the crow's nest ye look through your spyglass and see a horrid site. A british ship, lo! The Arrow! Headed straight for thee! Hurry down scallwag! And waste no time! Warn Captain Mumford to save your behinds! Or, drink some more and lighten up, you've had A long first day. The Scurvvy Kitten will survive, with a scrappy hard crew as this!
