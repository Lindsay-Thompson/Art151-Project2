Trouble on the Horizon

Ye feel squiffy after a long day of swabbing, singing chanteys, and sipping rum with the new mateys. This crew is like no other merchants you met before. And Captain Mumford is like no other Captains. Ye climb up to the crow's nest and look through ye new spyglass Only to spot a ship waiving the Union Jack about 5 leagues north of ye. Do ye Tell Captain Mumford that there is another British ship approachin
Ignore the ship and get back to drinkin
